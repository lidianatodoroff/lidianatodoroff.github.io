About Lidiana

Lidiana has played roles from dramatic shows such as The Outsiders to family fun musicals like Elf JR. working with broadway stars to constantly improve her skills and further her acting and modeling career. She’s best known for her comedic performance in The Brothers Grimm Spectaculathon as the lead.

Lidiana has worked with stars such as the Broadway actor, Sean Montgomery and model/voice actor, Jen Brissman as well as Lindsay Burden who has traveled internationally for The Young Americans.

Ms. Todoroff has been featured on the news for her generous contributions to her community. Starting in 5th grade Lidiana began her musical journey with the violin making it to All-State and OSAA competitions. She also plays cello, piano and most recently teaches music to younger students. She enjoys hiking, swimming, and a short run here and there but nothing will top her love of rock climbing. Lidiana was born and raised in Bend, Oregon but loves learning about new cultures and will travel the world someday, currently, she's taken two years of Japanese and an additional two years of Spanish. She’s looking forward to her next big accomplishment in her acting career.
